Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a Barangay Clearance?

To obtain a Barangay Certificate, you need to:

  1. Visit the Barangay Hall during office hours (8AM-5PM, Monday to Friday)
  2. Fill out the application form
  3. Go to Barangay Hall and pay the processing fee (depends on the type of Barangay Certificate you want to obtain)
  4. Wait for processing (usually within the same day)

How do I report a community concern?

You can report community concerns through:

  • The "Report" button on our website
  • Visiting the Barangay Hall in person
  • Calling our Barangay hotline at (049) 536-0047 or (049) 502-6234
  • Messaging our official Facebook page
